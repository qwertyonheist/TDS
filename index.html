Quick tab

Simple credits ↔ NPR converter including VAT. No TDS applied. Type in either the Credits or Amount field — the other becomes the output. Tap the × button to switch direction.

Credits → Amount

Enter the number of credits and rate to find the exact amount a client needs to pay after TDS is deducted.

Amount → Credits

Enter what a client actually paid (after TDS) to back-calculate how many SMS credits they should receive.

Tax logic

VAT is fixed at 13% applied on the base rate (excl. VAT).
TDS is deducted from the base amount only — not the VAT-inclusive total.
Net payable = (Base + VAT) − TDS.
